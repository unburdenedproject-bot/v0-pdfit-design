## Second Differentiator: No Login Wall

Every major competitor forces account creation before processing files.

We let users:
1. Upload without an account
2. Process their file
3. Download the result
4. Only then ask for signup (after 3 anonymous uses)

**Why this matters:** The #1 reason users abandon PDF tools is being forced to sign up before seeing results. We remove that friction entirely.

**The message:** "No account. No signup. Just upload and done."

---

## Third Differentiator: No Install, No Wait

Browser-based, instant processing. No desktop app to download, no plugin required. Works on any device including mobile.

**The message:** "Open your browser. Done in seconds."

## Fourth Differentiator: Trilingual from Day One

EN + ES + BR with native URL slugs and native SEO content (not machine-translated UI). Competitors have UI translations but almost zero native SEO depth in LATAM or Brazil.

**The message:** "Built for professionals across the Americas, not just translated for them."
