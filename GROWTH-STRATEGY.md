# PDF.it — Growth & Conversion Strategy

## Strategic Positioning

Do NOT market PDF.it as: "PDF tools"
Market it as: **"Fix any document problem instantly"**

Examples:
- Fix scan quality
- Prepare document for upload
- Extract data from PDFs
- Process hundreds of files

## Pricing Structure

| Tier | Price | Role |
|------|-------|------|
| Free | $0 | Traffic + acquisition |
| Pro | $7.99/mo | Main conversion tier |
| Business | $13.99/mo | Automation + batch |

## Free Tier Funnel

### Anonymous Users (no account)
- 3 document tasks
- Then show: "Create a free account to continue. You'll get 10 free tasks per day."
- Key rule: Do NOT show pricing yet — only the free upgrade

### Logged-In Free Users
- 10 tasks per day
- Single file processing
- Max file size 25MB
- Standard processing queue

### Soft Warning (after 8th task)
"You have 2 free tasks remaining today. Upgrade for unlimited processing."

### Hard Limit (after 10th task)
"You've reached your daily limit of 10 conversions. Upgrade to continue processing instantly."
- Button 1: Upgrade to Pro ($7.99)
- Button 2: Continue tomorrow

## Three Upgrade Triggers

1. **Usage limit** — "You reached today's limit. Upgrade to continue."
2. **File size limit** — "Files larger than 25MB require Pro."
3. **Batch processing** — "Batch processing is available on Business."

## Revenue Model

### Conservative (100k visitors/month)
- 1% conversion = 1,000 subscribers
- 700 Pro × $7.99 = $5,593
- 300 Business × $13.99 = $4,197
- Total: ~$10k/month

### Growth (300k visitors/month)
- 1.5% conversion = 4,500 subscribers
- 3,000 Pro × $7.99 = $23,970
- 1,500 Business × $13.99 = $20,985
- Total: ~$45k/month

## Traffic Model

Goal after 45 days: 80–120 SEO pages
Expected traffic: 100k–300k visitors/month
Typical conversion: 1–3%
Possible subscribers: 1k–5k

## Critical Rule

Success comes from:
- Consistent tool expansion
- Workflow automation
- SEO coverage

Not one feature.

## iLoveAPI Note for Compression Pages
- iLoveAPI compresses by level (low, recommended, extreme), NOT by exact target size
- For pages like /compress-pdf-to-5mb: use "recommended" first, check size, retry with "extreme" if still over 5MB
- If still over limit, show: "We reduced it as much as possible"
- Watch margins: iLoveAPI charges 10 credits per file
- When volume grows, replace with own Ghostscript worker for better margins
